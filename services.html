Our Services

Professional painting, rendering, and plastering solutions

Painting Services

We provide professional painting services for both residential and commercial properties. Using high-quality paints and precise techniques, we ensure a flawless finish that lasts.

What We Offer:

  • Interior Painting: Transform your indoor spaces with our expert interior painting services
  • Exterior Painting: Protect and enhance your property's exterior with weather-resistant paints
  • Commercial Painting: Professional painting for offices, retail spaces, and industrial facilities
  • Residential Painting: Quality painting services for homes and apartments
  • Wall Preparation: Proper surface preparation for optimal paint adhesion and longevity
  • Color Consultation: Help choosing the perfect colors for your space

Rendering Services

Our rendering services protect and beautify your property with various textures and finishes designed to withstand the elements while enhancing curb appeal.

What We Offer:

  • Acrylic Rendering: Flexible and crack-resistant finish with excellent durability
  • Texture Coating: Decorative textured finishes in various patterns
  • Cement Rendering: Traditional cement-based render for a solid, durable finish
  • Wall Repairs: Repair and restore damaged render surfaces
  • Surface Preparation: Proper cleaning and preparation for optimal adhesion
  • Custom Finishes: Specialized finishes to match your design preferences

Plastering Services

Our skilled plasterers deliver smooth finishes, perfect cornices, and precise architectural details that enhance the beauty and value of your property.

What We Offer:

  • Plasterboard Installation: Professional installation of plasterboard/gyprock
  • Cornice & Moulding: Installation of decorative cornices and architraves
  • Wall Repairs: Repair cracks, holes, and damaged plaster surfaces
  • Skimming: Applying a smooth skim coat over existing surfaces
  • Architectural Details: Custom plaster details and features
  • Ceiling Repairs: Fixing damaged or sagging ceilings
